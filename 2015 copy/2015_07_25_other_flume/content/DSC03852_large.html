Waterville Flume - the other flume

7/25/2015

A forecast of rain made us opt for an unusual and different random adventure that almost seems inspired by the idea of red lining. Taking the new Irene path into an area I had never been before... never heard of before. The trail was great, lots of signs of moose and we didn't bump into anyone once we got off the Livermore trail, although the actual Flume wasn't very impressive. This was my first day hiking with a macro lens - Minolta 100mm/2.8 with an adapter for the A7.
