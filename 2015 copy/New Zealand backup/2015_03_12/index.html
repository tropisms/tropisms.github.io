New Zealand: Arthur's Pass

3/12/2015

We started out with an overcast day and fog, but as we drove into the mountains it cleared up. The hike up Avalanche mountain probably the steepest I've ever done, the amount of sheer gain was impressive, and the last stretch at the top had steep enough cliffs to get my fear of heights going.
