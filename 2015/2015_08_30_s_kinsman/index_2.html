South Kinsman

8/30/2015

S Kinsman via the Reel Brook trail. This was my first time on this trail and what a pleasure it was. I think it might be the first time hiking in the whites where we were the only car at the trail head (and there wasn't anyone there when we finished either). In spite of that, the trail seemed strangely well maintained up to the AT. The section of the AT was wonderful, it followed a stream up a series of mossy waterfalls leading up to a pond. Then continuing up a series of scrambles which I thought were great, but might be a bit more than most people would like (not easy). This was my favorite way up the Kinsmans, although maybe best to spot a car and make a loop next time.
